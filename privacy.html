Who we are

BizSco ("we", "us", "our") is a free financial dashboard for Indian small business owners. We help you analyze cash flow, qualify for loans and grants, and make better financial decisions — all in a single browser-based tool with no app to install.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information when you use our website at https://ramaraju11.github.io/finwise-platform or our WhatsApp-based onboarding service.

BizSco operates from India. We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and offer protections aligned with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users worldwide.

What information we collect

Information you give us directly

  • WhatsApp number — when you start onboarding via WhatsApp
  • Business profile — business name, industry, monthly revenue band, biggest financial worry, and city (collected through 5 WhatsApp questions)
  • Financial inputs — figures you enter into our tools (e.g., monthly revenue, expenses, debt) to calculate insights. These stay in your browser by default; you can optionally save them to your account.
  • Payment information — if you subscribe, payment details are handled by Razorpay; we never see or store your card numbers or UPI IDs.
  • Email address — only if you provide it for support or GST invoicing

Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics — pages visited, tools used, anonymized via Google Analytics 4 (no personally identifying data sent)
  • Device info — browser type, OS, country (from IP, not stored)
  • Cookies — see Section 8

How we use your information

We use the data we collect only for these purposes:

  • To personalize your financial dashboard based on your answers
  • To send you onboarding messages and your personalized login link via WhatsApp
  • To process subscription payments through Razorpay
  • To recommend relevant grants, schemes, and tools (e.g., MUDRA, PMMY, CGTMSE) based on your industry and city
  • To improve our product through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., GST records, regulatory requests)
What we do NOT do: We do not sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or third parties. We do not share your financial data with lenders without your explicit consent. We do not use your data to train AI models.

WhatsApp Business use

We use the official WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API) from Meta to send and receive onboarding messages. When you sign up via WhatsApp:

  • Meta (WhatsApp's parent company) processes the message contents in order to deliver them — see WhatsApp's Privacy Policy
  • Your answers to the 5 onboarding questions are stored only on our servers, not in WhatsApp
  • We do not read your other WhatsApp conversations or contacts
  • You can opt out anytime by replying STOP in our WhatsApp chat

Storage and security

  • Your data is stored in Supabase (Postgres), encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.3
  • Access to the database is locked down by Row-Level Security (RLS); only authenticated Edge Functions can read your data
  • Magic-link tokens expire in 1 hour and are cryptographically random (190 bits of entropy)
  • WhatsApp message signatures are verified using HMAC-SHA256 to prevent forgery

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we erase all personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., GST invoicing records — kept for 8 years per Indian Income Tax Act).

Your rights (DPDP Act 2023 & GDPR)

Whether you're in India or elsewhere, you have these rights:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Fix anything that's inaccurate
  • Deletion ("right to be forgotten") — Request full erasure of your data
  • Portability — Get your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — Stop further processing (e.g., reply STOP on WhatsApp)
  • Grievance redressal — Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer or the Indian Data Protection Board

To exercise any right, email privacy@bizsco.in. We respond within 7 business days.

Cookies and similar tech

We use only essential cookies needed for the site to work:

  • localStorage — to remember your country, language, and tool inputs across visits (browser-side only; never sent to our servers without your action)
  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in during a session
  • Google Analytics — to count anonymous visits (you can opt out with browser do-not-track settings)

We do not use advertising cookies, social-media trackers, or third-party analytics beyond Google Analytics.

Children's data

BizSco is intended for business owners aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that a minor's data has been collected, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact privacy@bizsco.in if concerned.

Updates to this policy

We may revise this policy as our service evolves. If we make material changes, we'll notify users via WhatsApp (if you've signed up) and post a notice at the top of this page for at least 30 days. The "Last updated" date is shown at the top.
