Why both numbers? They look at your business from two different angles:
📊
Loan Risk Score answers: "If I add this loan, can my cash flow safely handle it?" Drops when DSCR or runway gets thin.
🩺
Business Health Grade answers: "How healthy is my business overall?" Stays steadier because it also considers profit margin — a single loan rarely tanks overall health.
💡
A business can be healthy overall (Grade B) but a specific loan can still be risky — like a fit person making a risky decision. Both can be true at the same time.
