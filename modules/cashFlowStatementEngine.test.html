How the three statements connect
P&L → Cash Flow Statement: Net Profit is the starting point for Operating CF (indirect method).
Loan → Cash Flow Statement: Monthly principal repayments from the amortization schedule feed into Financing CF.
Cash Flow Statement → Balance Sheet: Closing Cash from the CFS must match the Cash line on the Balance Sheet.
Balance Sheet → Cash Flow Statement: Changes in Balance Sheet working capital items (AR, Inventory, AP) drive the operating adjustments.
