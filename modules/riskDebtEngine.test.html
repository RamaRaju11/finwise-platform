Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)

DSCR = Net Operating Income ÷ Total Debt Service  |  Target: ≥ 1.25

Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)

DTI = (Total Monthly Debt ÷ Gross Monthly Income) × 100  |  Target: < 35%

Full Risk Assessment

Combines DSCR + DTI → overall risk level, alerts, and recommendations.
