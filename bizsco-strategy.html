Mobile-First Reality

India runs on phones. We do too.

95% of Indian SMB owners have never used a laptop for business. Every UX decision flows from this single fact.

Mobile-first choices we made

  • ✅ WhatsApp inside the product — onboarding lives in the app they already have
  • ✅ Vertical 9:16 layouts — designed for thumb-scrolling, not desktop clicking
  • ✅ Large tap targets (44px+) — works with sweaty fingers in summer humidity
  • ✅ Hindi · Telugu · Tamil · Marathi — UI auto-localizes (Hindi/Telugu live, Tamil/Marathi next)
  • ✅ 16px input font — prevents iOS auto-zoom on tap (small but critical)
  • ✅ Works on 2G/3G — pages under 100KB, no heavy frameworks
  • ✅ PWA-ready — installable as a home-screen icon, like an app
⚠ Open issue (reported 2026-06-03): some screens still too small on phones. Mobile audit pass-2 scheduled — see issue log in indiapending.md.
