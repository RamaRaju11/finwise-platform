Common questions

Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. We pro-rate the difference automatically — no awkward conversations with a sales team.
Is my financial data safe?
All your numbers stay in your browser and your private dashboard. We never see your bank statements. Payment processing is handled by Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified). We're GDPR + DPDP Act compliant.
Do I get a GST invoice?
Yes. Every subscription comes with a tax-compliant GST invoice emailed to your WhatsApp-linked email (or you can add an email anytime in settings).
What happens if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, you drop to the Free plan — your data stays intact. Re-subscribe anytime to unlock paid features again.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Free has no time limit — try it as long as you want. For Pro, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee: if it doesn't deliver value in your first week, email refunds@bizsco.in for a full refund, no questions asked.
