Frequently asked questions
Can I try before paying? ?
Yes — the Free plan gives you access to 5 core tools forever with no credit card. You can also open the demo at any time to explore all features before upgrading.
How does billing work? ?
Plans are billed monthly or annually via Stripe. You can cancel at any time — your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly.
Can I upgrade or downgrade? ?
Yes, you can upgrade anytime and the new plan activates immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Is my financial data safe? ?
BizSco processes your numbers in your browser. Data is saved locally to your device. We do not store your financial figures on any server.
What is the Advisor plan for? ?
The Advisor plan is built for accountants, CAs, and financial consultants who manage multiple business clients. It includes a multi-client dashboard, branded reports you can send to clients, referral tracking with commission estimates, and CA-ready exports.
Do I need to install anything? ?
No installation required. BizSco runs entirely in your web browser. It works on desktop and mobile — no app download needed.
