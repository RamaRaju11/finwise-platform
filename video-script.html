Recommended Workflow

Fastest path to a finished video (30–45 min total)

1

Open demo.html in Chrome, go fullscreen (press F). Set resolution to 1920×1080.

2

Start Loom or OBS recording. Capture the full browser window + microphone audio.

3

Press Play on the demo. Read each scene's voiceover text aloud as it plays (it auto-advances).

4

Stop recording. Trim the start/end in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve. Add captions if needed.

5

Optional: Paste this entire script into ElevenLabs for an AI voiceover. Sync to video in editor.

Production Tips

📣 Voiceover Pacing

Speak at ~130 words/minute — slower than conversation. The demo auto-advances, so match your pace to the scene timer shown in the controls bar. You can slow the demo to 0.5× speed for more reading time.

🖱 Mouse Movement

Keep your mouse still during recording unless you want to highlight something. Erratic movement is distracting. The demo is fully self-running — you don't need to click anything.

⌨ Keyboard Shortcuts (in demo.html)

Space / → : next scene · ← : previous scene · P : play/pause · F : fullscreen. Use these during recording if you need to pause and restart a scene.

🎵 Background Music

Add a subtle lo-fi or ambient track at 10–15% volume in your video editor. Try "Epidemic Sound" (free trial) or YouTube Audio Library for royalty-free options.
