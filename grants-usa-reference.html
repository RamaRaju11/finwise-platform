⚠️ Key Insight Before Reading True "interest-free" government loans are rare in the USA. Most SBA programs charge low interest (8–13%) — not zero. Real FREE money (grants) exist but are mostly for R&D businesses or specific categories (women, veterans, minorities). The biggest opportunity for FinWise is surfacing LOW-interest SBA loans that SMBs don't know exist — saving them from 18–24% private lender rates.

💡 FinWise Opportunity — SBA Guarantee The SBA doesn't lend directly — it guarantees loans made by approved banks. This means banks take less risk and approve more applications. Most SMBs don't know this and pay 18–24% at online lenders instead of 10–11% via SBA route. Surfacing this alone = massive value.

Section 6 — Where to Find All Schemes (Master Sources)

Use these to build the FinWise Grant Finder database

🏛 Grants.gov

Official federal grants database. Has a free API. Lists all federal grant opportunities across every agency. Best source for grant data.

grants.gov · API docs

🏦 SBA.gov

All SBA loan programs in one place. Has local assistance finder — shows SBDCs, SCORE mentors, and lenders by zip code.

sba.gov/funding-programs

🔬 SBIR.gov

All SBIR and STTR opportunities. Searchable by agency and technology area. Reauthorised through 2031.

sbir.gov

🗺 State Business Incentives Database

Best source for state-level grants, tax credits, and incentives. Searchable by state and business type.

selectusa.gov

🌾 USDA Rural Programs

Rural business grants and loans. Check eligibility by zip code. Often the most accessible for non-urban businesses.

rd.usda.gov

🤝 Hello Alice Platform

Aggregates corporate and private grants in one place. Free to join. Sends alerts when new grants match your profile.

helloalice.com/grants
